How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

SMS Messaging & Consent

By providing your phone number and opting in, you agree to receive:

Message frequency varies based on service schedule (typically 1–4 messages per week). Message & data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for assistance or email us at binpalsteam@gmail.com.
